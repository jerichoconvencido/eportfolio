Reflection

My Journey & Learnings

After our discussion, I realized how important the Cone of Experience, TPACK, SAMR, and the ASSURE model are for me as a future teacher. At first, these ideas seemed difficult, but as we talked about them, I understood how they can guide me in planning my lessons. The Cone of Experience reminded me that students learn better when they start with real and concrete activities before moving to symbols or theories. It also showed me that students have different ways of learning, so I should not force one method on everyone. The TPACK model helped me see that I must balance content, good teaching strategies, and the right use of technology. Knowing these concepts now makes me feel more prepared for the teaching profession.

As a future teacher, I also learned how SAMR and ASSURE can help me choose and use instructional materials in the right way. SAMR taught me that technology should improve learning and not just be used for show. The ASSURE model gave me a clear process on how to analyze my learners, set objectives, and prepare materials that fit their needs. I also became more aware of how important it is to understand my students‚Äô learning styles and attention span so I can adjust my lessons properly. This lesson made me appreciate how much effort teachers put into planning, and it inspired me to be more thoughtful and creative in my future classes. Overall, this discussion strengthened my desire to become a teacher who provides meaningful and effective learning experiences.
